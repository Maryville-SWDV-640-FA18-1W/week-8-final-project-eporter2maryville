My Name is Brent Porter. I was born and raised in Salt Lake City, Utah. Like many people from that great state, I was raised with a love for the outdoors and all they have to offer. That love has carried over into my photography. I began shooting at the age of 8 after being gifted a camera by my grandparents. I was immediately hooked, and have been tinking with pictures ever since.

My photography mostly revolves around my desire to get outside and explore the world around me, whehter that means backpacking through Yellowstone, wandering the streets of Paris, or just taking the time to step out in my own neck of the woods.

One of the things I love about photography is the ability to capture an emotion to share with others. This may be the feeling of wonder as the sun rises over Canyonlands, or the subtle peace that comes from hiking through a grove of aspens in the fall. My desire to share these emotions has led to my professional photography. I strive to share what I am able to create, so that others can convey their message.

I am available to contract for both commercial and private work. Various pieces of my work are on display here for purchase, but I am also available for contract work, both commercial and private. Please reach out if you have any questions.

I also contribute regularly on www.Medium.com. If you would like to read about some of my adventures in photography, please click the "Blog" link above.
